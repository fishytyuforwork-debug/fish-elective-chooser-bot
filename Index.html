Pick your electives
like you mean it.

認真選擇你的選修科。

Three short sections — your results, how you think, and what you actually enjoy — combine into a subject shortlist built for the HKDSE elective system. Takes about 6 minutes.

三個簡短部分 — 你的成績、你的思考方式，以及你真正感興趣的事物 — 結合成一份配合 HKDSE 選修制度的選科建議。大約需要 6 分鐘。

Before you start: the personality section is loosely based on MBTI-style questions. It's a fun way to reflect on how you work and think — not a scientific predictor of what subject you'll succeed in. Treat it as one signal among several, not the answer. Your actual results and genuine interest matter more.
開始之前：性格部分參考 MBTI 形式的問題，是反思自己思考及行事方式的有趣方法 — 並非科學化的學業表現預測工具。請將它視為眾多參考之一，而非絕對答案。你的實際成績和真實興趣更為重要。

Section 2 / 3 · 第二部分

Pick the option that feels more like you — there's no wrong answer, and it's fine if neither feels perfect. Go with your gut.

Please answer every question before continuing. 請回答所有問題後再繼續。
